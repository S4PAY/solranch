Sol Ranch — Game Guide

The First Virtual Ranch for Degens on Solana. Hold $RANCH, complete tasks, earn weekly USDC.

Overview

Sol Ranch is a gamified DeFi platform on Solana. You paste your wallet address, complete daily ranch tasks, hold $RANCH tokens, and earn weekly USDC payouts from real trading volume.

There are no smart contracts to trust, no VC unlocks, and no token emissions. All rewards are funded by creator fees from $RANCH trades on Pump.fun.

The core loop: Hold $RANCH → Complete daily tasks → Earn points → Get paid USDC every Monday → Sellers forfeit their share to diamond hands.

How to Join

1. Go to solranch.farm/app

2. Paste your Solana wallet address (Phantom, Solflare, or any SPL wallet)

3. Your ranch is created instantly. No wallet connection, no gas fees, no minting.

4. Buy at least 50,000 $RANCH on Pump.fun to unlock tasks and reward eligibility.

Your keys stay safe. Sol Ranch never asks for wallet connection or seed phrases. You only paste your public address.

$RANCH Token

$RANCH is the native token of Sol Ranch, trading on Pump.fun. It serves three purposes:

1. Reward eligibility — Hold 50k+ $RANCH all week to qualify for USDC payouts.

2. Pen unlocks — Higher holdings unlock more animal pens with more earning potential.

3. Raid access — You need $RANCH to raid other ranches and steal points.

30% of all creator trading fees are used to buy $RANCH and burn it. 70% goes to the weekly USDC reward pool.

Animal Pens

Animal Pens are mini-businesses on your ranch. Each pen has unique animals, tasks, and special abilities. Unlock more pens by holding more $RANCH.

Important: Daily Chores (checkin, water, patrol, evening feed, social share) are always available and separate from pen tasks. Pen tasks are bonus earning on top of your daily chores. You do both every day.

Health System

Each pen has a health bar (0-100%). Health drops 15% per day automatically. If health drops below 50%, tasks earn half points. At 0%, you earn nothing from that pen.

Restore health by completing feed and care tasks, or buy supplies from the Supply Shop.

Ranch Raids

Attack other ranches to steal their points. Go to the Rodeo tab, pick a target, and hit Raid.

HoldingRaids per DayTier

Win: Steal 10% of the target's daily points (minimum 20 points).

Lose: Forfeit 100 points. No refund.

Success chance depends on your hold tier versus theirs. Higher tier = higher chance. Your streak also boosts raid success.

Shield: After being raided, you get a 3-hour shield. Nobody can attack you during that time. If someone raids you and fails, you earn 25 bonus defense points.

Complete all daily chores, earn 100+ points in a day, do both morning and evening feed, keep your streak alive, and more.

Eligibility Rules

To qualify for weekly USDC payouts, you must meet ALL of these conditions:

1. Hold at least 50,000 $RANCH for at least 5 of 7 days during the week.
2. Complete at least one active task (check-in, social, raid, or buy) during the week.
3. Have your wallet registered on the site (pasted your address).

Sell = Forfeit. If your balance drops below 50k on more than 2 days during the week, your entire weekly reward is forfeited. Your share gets redistributed to everyone who held.

Just holding is not enough. You must actively participate by completing at least one task.

Treasury

The reward treasury accumulates USDC from trading fees throughout the week. The live balance is displayed on the Rewards tab and landing page.

Every Monday, the full treasury is distributed automatically to eligible holders based on their weekly points share. 70% of creator fees fund the treasury. 30% buys and burns $RANCH.

Treasury is funded by 70% of creator trading fees from $RANCH on Pump.fun. The remaining 30% buys and burns $RANCH.

Auto-Buy & Burn

When creator fees accumulate to $20 worth of SOL:

30% is used to buy $RANCH on the open market and burn it permanently. The tokens are sent to the Solana incinerator address and can never be recovered.

70% is converted to USDC and deposited into the weekly reward treasury.

Every burn is logged with a Solscan transaction link, visible on the Rewards tab. Burns can happen multiple times per day depending on trading volume.

How to Purchase

1. Open the Supply Shop in any unlocked pen.

2. Select the item you want.

3. Send the required $RANCH to the burn address in your Phantom wallet.

4. Paste the transaction signature back into the app.

5. The system verifies on-chain that the burn happened from your wallet, with the correct amount.

Anti-cheat: The burn address, sender wallet, token, and amount are all verified on-chain via Helius. Each transaction can only be used once. You cannot reuse a TX or fake a burn.
