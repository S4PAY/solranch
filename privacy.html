Sol Ranch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Sol Ranch is a gamified DeFi platform on Solana. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the Solana wallet address you voluntarily provide when entering the ranch. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

How We Use Information

Your wallet address is used to track your ranch activity, points, streaks, and reward eligibility. On-chain token balances are read via public blockchain data through the Helius RPC API.

Data Storage

Ranch data (points, streaks, task completions) is stored in a secure database on our server. We do not store private keys, seed phrases, or any wallet authentication credentials.

Third-Party Services

We use Helius RPC to read on-chain Solana data. We do not share your wallet address or activity data with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Cookies

We use local storage to remember your wallet address for convenience. No tracking cookies are used.

Children's Privacy

Sol Ranch is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, reach us on Telegram or X (Twitter).
