The Trail

How It Works

$RANCH trades on Pump.fun. Every trade generates creator fees. Those fees fund weekly USDC rewards for ranchers who hold $RANCH and grind daily tasks. 30% of fees buy and burn $RANCH.

01

PASTE YOUR WALLET

Paste your Solana wallet address. Your ranch is created instantly. No wallet connection, no minting fees, no gas. Your keys stay safe.

02

WORK YOUR RANCH

Hold at least 50k $RANCH and complete daily tasks. Feed cattle, repair fences, raid other ranches. Points accumulate all week toward your Monday payday.

03

EARN WEEKLY USDC

Every Monday, the full USDC treasury is distributed to eligible holders based on weekly points. Sell your $RANCH during the week? Rewards forfeited and redistributed to diamond hands.

04

CLIMB THE RANKS

As your lifetime points grow, your ranch levels up. Higher ranks mean bigger multipliers on everything you earn. Cattle Barons earn 3x.
